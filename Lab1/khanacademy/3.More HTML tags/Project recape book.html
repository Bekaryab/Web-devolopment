Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1 package
Bacon 150g
Eggs 3

Step 1: Fry the bacon until crispy.

Step 2: Boil the pasta according to the instructions.

Step 3: Mix the eggs and bacon with the pasta.

Step 4: Serve with grated cheese.

Classic Bolognese

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef 500g
Onions 2
Tomato Paste 2 tbsp

Step 1: Brown the beef in a pan.

Step 2: Add the onions and cook until translucent.

Step 3: Stir in the tomato paste and simmer for 45 minutes.

Step 4: Serve with spaghetti or your favorite pasta.

Vegetable Polo

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1 cup
Carrots 1
Green Beans 100g

Step 1: Cook the rice in a pot with water and salt.

Step 2: Stir-fry the vegetables.

Step 3: Mix the rice and vegetables together.

Step 4: Serve hot with your favorite sauce.
